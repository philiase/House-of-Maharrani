Mission

HOM's mission is to elevate women's wellbeing with love and kindness.

Vision

We hope to create a safe space for women where women can gather together to make conscious changes which directly impact their connection to self and others. We hope to encourage conversations and experiences that help, and empower them to make the changes needed to build a world for self, others, and nature with love and kindness.
